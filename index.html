I am a linguist. I am currently an assistant professor at the department of Spanish, Modern and Classical Languages of the a href="https://www.uib.eu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Universitat de les Illes Balears.

Before that I have worked as a computational linguist at Google (through Adecco Outsourcing) and I was a lecturer at the department of Language Theory and Communication Sciences of the Universitat de València and at the department of Spanish Linguistics and Literary Theory of the Universidad Complutense de Madrid.

I received my PhD in Cognitive Science and Language from the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB). My dissertation Adverbial adjectives and nominal scalarity was written at the Linguistics and Cognitive Science research group (LyCC) of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC).

My research focuses on the semantics and pragmatics of modification, especially regarding gradability, vagueness and subjectivity, and on language acquisition. On the natural language processing side, I try to apply my background as formal semanticist to sentiment analysis.

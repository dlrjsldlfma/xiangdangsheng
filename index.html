I am a linguist. I am currently a lecturer at the department of Language Theory and Communication Sciences of the Universitat de València. I also work as a computational linguist at Google (through Adecco Outsourcing).

I received my PhD in Cognitive Science and Language from the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB). My dissertation Adverbial adjectives and nominal scalarity was written at the Linguistics and Cognitive Science research group (LyCC) of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC).

My research focuses on the semantics and pragmatics of modification, especially regarding gradability, vagueness and subjectivity. On the natural language processing side, I try to apply my background as formal semanticist to sentiment analysis.
